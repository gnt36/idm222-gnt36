Welcome To A Peak Into My Life

My name is Gia. The purpose of this website is to showcase my life and spread awareness about the importance of mental health, as well as how I get through everyday life with my diagnosis.

I have been struggling with my own mental health for several years now. I am currently diagnosed with an unspecified mood disorder. Basically what that means is I struggle with emotional regulation and staying in a wise mind in respect to relationships.

Please browse around my site and contact me with any questions or concerns!
